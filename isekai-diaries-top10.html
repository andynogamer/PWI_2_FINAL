Top 10 Isekai Anime You Must Watch in 2025

By Fatima April 28, 2025 10.2K Views

The year 2025 is shaping up to be another golden year for isekai genre lovers. With an avalanche of new series, anticipated sequels, and adaptations of popular light novels, choosing what to watch can be overwhelming. But fear not! We've compiled a list of the 10 isekai anime you simply can't miss this year.

From epic fantasy worlds to hilarious comedies about being transported to strange places, this list has something for everyone. Prepare your snacks and get comfortable, because these are our top recommendations for your 2025 isekai marathon.

Why Does Isekai Continue to Captivate?

The appeal of isekai is undeniable. The idea of escaping the monotony of everyday life and embarking on an adventure in a new and exciting world resonates deeply. Furthermore, the genre offers incredible versatility, allowing for stories ranging from unrestrained action and powerful magic to the exploration of complex social dynamics and the protagonist's personal development.

Our Top 10 Picks for 2025:

  1. Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World (Season 4)

    Subaru Natsuki returns with more suffering and heartbreaking challenges. This season promises to adapt one of the most intense and acclaimed arcs of the light novel, taking character development and psychological tension to new levels.

  2. Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation (Season 3)

    The continuation of Rudeus Greyrat's adventures. With consistently spectacular animation and a mature narrative, this season will explore new regions of the vast world and introduce crucial characters for Rudeus's future.

  3. KonoSuba: God's Blessing on This Wonderful World! (New Movie)

    The most dysfunctional group of adventurers in isekai is back! Get ready for more explosions, ridiculous situations, and the inimitable comedy that only Kazuma, Aqua, Megumin, and Darkness can deliver in this new cinematic adventure.

  4. Overlord (Season 5)

    Ainz Ooal Gown continues his conquest of the New World. We expect to see more complex strategies, overwhelming power demonstrations, and the expansion of Nazarick's domain. Which new kingdoms will fall before the Sorcerer King?

  5. The Eminence in Shadow (Season 2)

    Cid Kagenou, aka Shadow, continues to pull the strings from the shadows (or so he thinks). This season promises more epic misunderstandings, stylized action, and the expansion of the mysterious Cult of Diablos.

  6. Saga of Tanya the Evil (New Season/Movie)

    The relentless Tanya Degurechaff returns to the battlefield. Whether it's a new season or a movie, fans can expect more cunning military strategy, intense warfare action, and Tanya's cynical reflections on war and faith.

  7. Ascendance of a Bookworm (Season 4)

    Myne (now Rozemyne) continues her quest for books in a world where they are a luxury. This season will delve deeper into politics, nobility, and the challenges of introducing printing to a medieval society.

  8. That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime (Movie or Season 4)

    Rimuru Tempest and the Jura Tempest Federation face new threats and forge new alliances. Nation-building, diplomacy, and spectacular battles will continue to be the core of this popular franchise.

  9. New Original Isekai from Studio Trigger

    Although details are scarce, the renowned Studio Trigger (Kill la Kill, Cyberpunk: Edgerunners) has announced an original isekai project. With their dynamic visual style and bold narratives, this is one of the most anticipated for its innovative potential.

  10. Isekai Ojisan (Season 2)

    After the success of its first season, Takafumi's uncle might return with more stories from his 17 years in the fantasy world of Granbahamal, mixing nostalgic SEGA comedy with inverted isekai tropes.

"2025 is shaping up to be a year where isekai will not only deliver quantity but also impressive quality and diversity. From sequels of established giants to intriguing new proposals, there's a portal to another world waiting for every type of fan."
Anime Horizon Magazine

This is just a sample of what 2025 has in store for isekai enthusiasts. Which of these titles are you most looking forward to? Is there any you think should be on the list? Share your opinions in the comments!
