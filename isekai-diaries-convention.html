Isekai Convention Announced for Summer 2025

By Angel April 15, 2025 4.2K Views

Get ready to be transported, isekai fans! Organizers today officially announced the inaugural **"Otherworldly Journeys: The Isekai Convention,"** a brand-new event dedicated entirely to the beloved genre of being whisked away to fantastical new worlds. The convention is scheduled to take place in Tokyo during the summer of 2025, promising an unforgettable experience for attendees.

This marks the first large-scale convention solely focused on isekai anime, manga, light novels, and games. The event aims to bring together fans, creators, voice actors, and industry professionals to celebrate the diverse and ever-expanding isekai universe.

What to Expect at "Otherworldly Journeys 2025"

The organizers have teased a packed schedule featuring a wide array of activities and attractions. While the full guest list and detailed program are yet to be revealed, attendees can look forward to:

  • Guest Panels & Q&A Sessions: Opportunities to hear from renowned isekai authors, illustrators, anime directors, and voice actors.
  • Exclusive Screenings: Premieres of new isekai anime episodes or even sneak peeks of upcoming projects.
  • Cosplay Competitions: A chance for fans to showcase their creativity by dressing up as their favorite isekai characters.
  • Artist Alley & Exhibitor Hall: Discover unique fan art, official merchandise, and works from independent creators.
  • Interactive Workshops: Learn about world-building, character design, or even try your hand at Japanese voice acting.
  • Themed Cafes & Photo Ops: Immerse yourself in settings inspired by popular isekai worlds.

A Celebration of a Genre

The convention director, Haruto Sato, shared his excitement: "The isekai genre has captured the hearts of millions globally. It offers escapism, adventure, and often, profound character journeys. 'Otherworldly Journeys' is our way of creating a dedicated space for this passionate community to come together and celebrate the stories that transport us."

The choice of Tokyo as the host city is fitting, given its status as the heart of anime culture. The event is expected to draw both local fans and international visitors, further solidifying isekai's global appeal.

"We envision 'Otherworldly Journeys' not just as a convention, but as a portal itself – a place where fans can step into the magic of their favorite isekai series for a few unforgettable days."
Haruto Sato, Convention Director

Ticket sales and further details about "Otherworldly Journeys: The Isekai Convention 2025" are expected to be announced in the coming months. Isekai enthusiasts should keep a close eye on the official convention website and social media channels for updates. This is one summer event you won't want to be reincarnated out of!
